Strategic Rationale

Why Propose This Now?

National Digital Imperative

The UAE Strategy for Industry 4.0 and Operation 300bn are accelerating industrial digitalization. With 94% of the population accessing the internet via smartphones — the highest rate globally — mobile is the primary interface customers expect.

Smart Dubai halo effect raises B2B expectations

Competitive Vacuum

Premium interior manufacturers in the UAE — whether local or international — continue to rely on websites, phone calls, emails, and showroom visits. None offer a dedicated customer mobile app with real-time project tracking, quote requests, and showroom booking.

Zero direct competitors with mobile customer apps
⚠️

Risk of Inaction

Faham's consultative sales model creates long sales cycles. Without digital tracking, customers lose visibility into project status. Paper-based quotes and fragmented WhatsApp communication create audit gaps and lost leads.

Quote-to-order conversion at risk
